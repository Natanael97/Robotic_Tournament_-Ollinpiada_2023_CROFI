# Robotics Competition Organization – CROFI

## Overview
This repository documents my role as President of the Robotics Club (CROFI) at UNAM, where I led the organization of a robotics competition.

## Objective
To design and execute a robotics competition that promoted innovation, teamwork, and engineering skills among students.

## My Role
- Event planning and coordination
- Stakeholder management
- Sponsorship acquisition
- Team leadership

This repository includes supporting materials such as:
- Event presentations
- Planning documents
- Promotional materials
- Photographic evidence
